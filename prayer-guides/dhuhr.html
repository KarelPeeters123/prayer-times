Dhuhr Prayer Guide

Dhuhr is the midday prayer, performed after the sun passes its zenith. It consists of 4 Rak'ah and is the second obligatory prayer of the day.

Welcome to Dhuhr Prayer

This interactive guide will take you through the Dhuhr prayer step by step.

Time: From when the sun passes its zenith until mid-afternoon

Rak'ah: 4 (recited silently)

Click "Next" when you're ready to begin.

1. Preparation (Wudu)

Ensure you have performed Wudu (ablution) and are facing the Qibla (direction of Kaaba in Mecca).

Stand with your feet slightly apart, hands at your sides.

Make sure you're clean and in a clean place.

Click "Next" when you're ready to make the intention.
