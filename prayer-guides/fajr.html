🌅 Fajr Prayer Guide

Fajr is the dawn prayer, performed before sunrise. It consists of 2 Rak'ah and is one of the most blessed prayers of the day.

Welcome to Fajr Prayer

This interactive guide will take you through the Fajr prayer step by step.

Time: From true dawn (Fajr) until just before sunrise

Rak'ah: 2

Click "Next" when you're ready to begin.

1. Preparation (Wudu)

Ensure you have performed Wudu (ablution) and are facing the Qibla (direction of Kaaba in Mecca).

Stand with your feet slightly apart, hands at your sides.

Make sure you're clean and in a clean place.

Click "Next" when you're ready to make the intention.

2. Intention (Niyyah)
Make the intention in your heart to perform the Fajr prayer for Allah.
نَوَيْتُ أَنْ أُصَلِّيَ فَرْضَ الْفَجْرِ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Nawaytu an usalliya farda al-fajri lillahi ta'ala
"I intend to pray the Fajr obligation for Allah"
3. Opening Takbir
Raise your hands to ear level and say "Allahu Akbar"
اللَّهُ أَكْبَر
Allahu Akbar
"Allah is Most Great"
Then place your hands over your chest (right hand over left).
4. Opening Supplication
Recite the opening supplication:
سُبْحَانَكَ اللَّهُمَّ وَبِحَمْدِكَ وَتَبَارَكَ اسْمُكَ وَتَعَالَى جَدُّكَ وَلَا إِلَهَ غَيْرُك
Subhanaka Allahumma wa bihamdika wa tabaraka ismuka wa ta'ala jadduka wa la ilaha ghayruk
"Glory be to You, O Allah, and praise be to You. Blessed is Your name, exalted is Your majesty, and there is no god but You."
5. Recite Al-Fatiha
Recite Surah Al-Fatiha (required in every rak'ah):
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
Bismillahi-r-Rahmani-r-Raheem
"In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful"
5. Al-Fatiha (continued)
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
Alhamdu lillahi rabbil-alameen
"All praise is due to Allah, Lord of the worlds"
الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
Ar-Rahmani-r-Raheem
"The Most Gracious, the Most Merciful"
5. Al-Fatiha (final verses)
مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ
Maliki yawmi-d-deen
"Master of the Day of Judgment"
إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ
Iyyaka na'budu wa iyyaka nasta'een
"You alone we worship, and You alone we ask for help"
5. Al-Fatiha (conclusion)
اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ
Ihdinas-siratal-mustaqeem
"Guide us to the straight path"
صِرَاطَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ غَيْرِ الْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا الضَّالِّينَ
Siratal-ladhina an'amta alayhim ghayril-maghdubi alayhim wa la-d-dalleen
"The path of those whom You have blessed, not of those who have incurred Your wrath, nor of those who have gone astray"
6. Additional Surah
Now recite an additional surah. Here's Al-Kawthar:
إِنَّا أَعْطَيْنَاكَ الْكَوْثَرَ
Inna a'taynakal-kawthar
"Indeed, We have granted you abundance"
فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَانْحَرْ
Fasalli li-rabbika wan-har
"So pray to your Lord and sacrifice"
7. Ruku (Bowing)
Say "Allahu Akbar" and bow down. Place hands on knees and say:
سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الْعَظِيم
Subhana Rabbiyal Azeem
"Glory be to my Lord, the Most Great"
Repeat 3 times minimum.
8. Standing After Ruku
Stand up straight and say:
سَمِعَ اللَّهُ لِمَنْ حَمِدَه، رَبَّنَا لَكَ الْحَمْد
Sami'a Allahu liman hamidah, Rabbana lakal hamd
"Allah hears whoever praises Him. Our Lord, praise be to You"
9. First Sujud (Prostration)
Say "Allahu Akbar" and prostrate. Place forehead, nose, palms, knees, and toes on the ground. Say:
سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الْأَعْلَى
Subhana Rabbiyal A'la
"Glory be to my Lord, the Most High"
Repeat 3 times minimum.
10. Sitting Between Sujud
Say "Allahu Akbar" and sit briefly between the two prostrations.
You may say: "Rabbighfir li" (My Lord, forgive me)
Then prepare for the second prostration.
11. Second Sujud
Say "Allahu Akbar" and prostrate again:
سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الْأَعْلَى
Subhana Rabbiyal A'la
"Glory be to my Lord, the Most High"
This completes the first rak'ah. Now stand for the second rak'ah.
12. Second Rak'ah
Say "Allahu Akbar" and stand up for the second rak'ah.
Repeat the same process:
• Recite Al-Fatiha
• Recite additional surah
• Perform Ruku and Sujud
After the second rak'ah, sit for Tashahhud.
13. Tashahhud (Sitting)
After the second rak'ah, sit and recite:
التَّحِيَّاتُ لِلَّهِ وَالصَّلَوَاتُ وَالطَّيِّبَاتُ، السَّلَامُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا النَّبِيُّ وَرَحْمَةُ اللَّهِ وَبَرَكَاتُه
At-tahiyyatu lillahi wa-salawatu wa-tayyibatu. As-salamu alayka ayyuhan-nabiyyu wa rahmatu Allahi wa barakatuh
"All greetings, prayers and pure words are for Allah. Peace be upon you, O Prophet, and Allah's mercy and blessings"
13. Tashahhud (continued)
السَّلَامُ عَلَيْنَا وَعَلَى عِبَادِ اللَّهِ الصَّالِحِين، أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّه، وَأَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمَّداً عَبْدُهُ وَرَسُولُه
As-salamu alayna wa ala ibadi Allahi-saliheen. Ashhadu an la ilaha illa Allah, wa ashhadu anna Muhammadan abduhu wa rasuluh
"Peace be upon us and upon the righteous servants of Allah. I bear witness that there is no god but Allah, and I bear witness that Muhammad is His servant and messenger"
14. Durood (Blessings)
Send blessings upon Prophet Muhammad (PBUH):
اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّد، كَمَا صَلَّيْتَ عَلَى إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَعَلَى آلِ إِبْرَاهِيم، إِنَّكَ حَمِيدٌ مَجِيد
Allahumma salli ala Muhammad wa ala ali Muhammad, kama sallayta ala Ibrahim wa ala ali Ibrahim, innaka Hameedum Majeed
"O Allah, send prayers upon Muhammad and his family, as You sent prayers upon Ibrahim and his family. Indeed, You are Praiseworthy and Glorious"
15. Taslim (Ending)
Turn your head to the right and say:
السَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُمْ وَرَحْمَةُ اللَّه
As-salamu alaykum wa rahmatu Allah
"Peace be upon you and Allah's mercy"
Then turn to the left and repeat the same.
🎉 Prayer Completed!

Congratulations! You have completed the Fajr prayer.

May Allah accept your prayer and grant you His blessings.

You may now make personal du'a or perform dhikr (remembrance of Allah).
